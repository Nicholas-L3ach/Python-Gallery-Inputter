While I love all different forms of art, traditional art is where my heart truly lies. Something about the tactility of a real painting, drawing, or sculpture makes excites my brain. I love the idea that if you use basic materials in a very purposeful way you can create something of astounding beauty and inspiration. I have done many drawings in my time but truly what I have worked up towards is oil painting. You will see a few oil paintings as well as acrylic in this gallery. There are some paintings that are extremely colorful matching the design of this website, those are my artworks done with POSCA paint pens.
